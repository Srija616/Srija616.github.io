Swabha Swayamdipta

Postdoctoral Investigator • MOSAIC • Allen Institute for AI

My research focuses on studying biases in datasets and models - the good, the bad and the ugly. Good biases, such as structural inductive biases are beneficial for language understanding, I wrote a PhD thesis on these. Bad biases arise from spurious correlations commonly found in crowd-sourced, large-scale datasets due to annotation artifacts and have unintended effects. And finally, biases can be ugly, when they stem from social prejudices of human annotators and task designers (coming soon!).
